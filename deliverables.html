Expected Outcomes and Deliverables Timeline

Phase 1 (Months 1–4): Foundation and Planning

  • Requirements analysis and system architecture design
  • Partner coordination and resource allocation
  • Initial technology assessment and integration planning

Phase 2 (Months 5–10): Development and Implementation

  • Core system development and component integration
  • SIEM and SDN platform implementation
  • UAV network deployment and testing
  • Security system integration and testing

Phase 3 (Months 11–14): Integration and Validation

  • System integration and comprehensive testing
  • Performance evaluation and optimization
  • Final deliverable preparation and documentation
  • Dissemination and exploitation planning

Publication and Dissemination Deliverables

  • Minimum 4 publications in international conferences
  • Minimum 2 publications in international journals
  • Patent application preparation and submission
  • Project website with regular updates and results
  • Technical demonstrations and stakeholder presentations

Commercial Exploitation Deliverables

  • Technology transfer documentation and procedures
  • Commercial application roadmaps and business cases
  • Intellectual property protection and licensing strategies
  • Partnership agreements for future development and deployment
