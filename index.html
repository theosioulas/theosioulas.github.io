Advanced Secure Telecommunications Infrastructure

RESPOND: Developing Secure Telecommunications Infrastructure using Satellite, Aerial and Terrestrial Technology

The RESPOND project aims to enhance and extend GOVSATCOM networks by developing innovative technical solutions that integrate satellite, aerial, and terrestrial communication technologies into a unified, robust, and secure communication infrastructure.

Project Overview

The RESPOND project addresses the critical need to improve and expand GOVSATCOM networks beyond traditional satellite communication limits. Our research focuses on four main axes:

  1. Satellite Infrastructure Extension – Integrating GEO and LEO satellites with UAVs and terrestrial infrastructures
  2. Software-Defined Radio (SDR) – Creating innovative aerial 5G‑NTN networks
  3. SIEM Platform – Advanced security monitoring and threat detection
  4. Software-Defined Networking (SDN) – Intelligent network management and optimization
